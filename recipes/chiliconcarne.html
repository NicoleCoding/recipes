Chili Con Carne

Description

Chili con carne is a great dish, with lots of spiciness. It can be served with a variety of sides, such as nachos, rice or tortillas.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 500g beef mince
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 800g crushed tomatoes, canned
  • 420g red kidney beans, canned
  • Salt and pepper
  • 125-375ml water
  • 1-2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 4 tsp paprika powder
  • 5 tsp cumin powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp oregano

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add garlic and onion, cook for 1 minute, then add bell pepper and cook for 2 minutes until onion is translucent.
  2. Turn heat up to high and add beef. Cook, breaking it up as you go, until mostly browned.
  3. Add the spices to the beef and cook until it is all browned.
  4. Add the remaining ingredients and bring to simmer.
  5. Slow cook for 1.5 to 2 hours, covered and on low heat.
  6. Salt and pepper to taste.
